Test Vanilla Model

Description

In this notebook, we show the performance of the vanilla model with 3 layers (where sensitivity datsets have been combined into a single matrix to increase overlap). This is essentially the new benchmark to beat in terms of auc. We will evaluate the performance on the various drug target datasets. We ignore dtc for now since it’s not very specific.
